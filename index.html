About Sunlip

Sunlip is a record label that aims to push music from younger artists and newcomers to the scene who are experimenting with their own sounds, inspired by the UK electronic underground (particularly the currently-booming UK garage scene).

At the core of our sound is the fun of the dance - there's already some really exciting focus on this in from other labels in the scene. Where 'fun' subgenres like speed garage and hard house have blown up in popularity in the last few years and have therefore benefitted from more label coverage, there's a gap for a platform that encourages newer producers to branch out.

We've all enjoyed sets that blend in cross-genre mixups, lone 2-steppers amongst the 4x4, internationally-inspired grooves, and creative (but tasteful) bootlegs. Tracks that stand out from the rest - that's the music that Sunlip aims to encourage.
